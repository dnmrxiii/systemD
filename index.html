Featured Episodes

"Three Robots" is a humorous and insightful exploration of a post-apocalyptic world, seen through the eyes of three robots on a sightseeing tour. This episode blends wit and satire to examine the remnants of human civilization.

"The Witness" is a visually stunning and intense thriller that follows a woman who witnesses a murder and is subsequently pursued by the killer through a surreal, neon-lit cityscape. The episode's unique animation style and gripping narrative make it a standout.

"Jibaro" tells the story of a deaf knight who encounters a golden woman in a mystical forest. This visually captivating episode delves into themes of greed, desire, and the consequences of human actions on the natural world, featuring mesmerizing animation and a haunting storyline.
